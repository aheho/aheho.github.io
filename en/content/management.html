Management

aheho was founded in Paris, France by Olivier Buigues and Alexis Vassilopoulos, two friends who know each other for more than 20 years. After following independent paths, they decided to create aheho. 

 

Olivier Buigues is co-founder of aheho. Olivier has 7 years of management experience in product management, marketing strategy and sales. He worked in multinationals as well as in small businesses. Olivier has a Masters in Management and a BA in economics.

 

 

Alexis Vassilopoulos is the Operational and Financial director of aheho. Alexis has 6 years of management experience in product development, computer engineering, strategic consulting and operations. Alexis has an MBA and a BA in computer science.
